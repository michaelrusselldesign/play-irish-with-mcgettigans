About PlayIrish with McGettigan's

PLAY IRISH is an IASCA initiative to support and celebrate Irish music of all genres both at home and internationally.


We’re delighted to partner with McGettigans worldwide to bring even more Irish music to their customers. Songs are chosen by the professionals, Irish radio DJ’s, TV music bookers, Press journalists - the ones in the know!

Featured Artist

Saint Sister

http://www.saintsisterband.com

Tinkering on the brink of world domination, Saint Sister are friends Morgan and Gemma, who formed in November 2014 . They released their debut EP Madrid on Trout Records in 2015 and have been gaining momentum ever since. Live they are a thing of beauty with haunting melodies and harmonies. They appeared in the current series of Other Voices TV, played the Hard Working Heroes festival and have just returned from the SXSW festival in Austin. Their music draws from early Celtic harp traditions, 60s folk and electronic pop to create ‘atmosfolk'—a mix of soulful vocal harmonies, dreamy synth and electro-acoustic harp.

‘Theirs is a strong, resonant, magnetic sound, with a clear folk influence in the wash, but one where the spooky, full-bodied songs they’re digging out also come with a lot of melodic gumption' — Jim Carroll, Irish Times
